## What M5 delivered (the thing being audited)

A **second native platform, in fact**: the same Blazor app runs interactively on the
iOS simulator (Swift/UIKit shell over a NativeAOT `.a`) AND on the Android AVD
(Kotlin/JNA shell over a bionic `.so`), from one runtime. Plus host-initiated events
(lifecycle/back/deep-link), clipboard/share, and a documented size-negotiated
bridge-extension pattern. Six phases (5.0–5.4) shipped; 5.5 closes.
